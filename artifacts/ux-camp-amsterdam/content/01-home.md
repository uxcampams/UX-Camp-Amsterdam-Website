# UXcamp Amsterdam 2025

**Source URL:** https://uxcampams.com/

---

## Event Overview

UXcamp Amsterdam is a community-driven BarCamp focused on User Experience Design and Research. There are **no spectators, only contributors** — it's an unconference-style gathering where attendees shape the programme.

---

## Key Details

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Date** | June 21, 2025 |
| **Venue** | HvA Corry Tendeloohuis (CTH), Fraijlemaborg 133, Amsterdam |
| **Hours** | 9:30 AM – 5:15 PM |

---

## Schedule Highlights

- Registration opens
- Morning **Madness Session** — participants pitch ideas
- Multiple discussion rounds throughout the day
- Lunch
- Afternoon break
- Closing drinks & after-party

---

## How to Participate

### Run a Session
Propose a talk, workshop, or discussion. The audience votes on proposals during the morning session to determine the day's schedule.

### Volunteer
Support the event through setup assistance, registration help, and cleanup.

### Community
Share your experience using **#UXcampAMS25**

---

## Organisation

Hosted by the **Communication & Multimedia Design** programme at the University of Applied Sciences Amsterdam.
Giveaways provided by the International Design Foundation.

The organising team includes UX designers, researchers, and freelancers from the Amsterdam design community.
