# UXcamp Amsterdam 2018

**Source URL:** https://uxcampams.com/welcome

---

## Overview

UXcamp Amsterdam 2018 was a **free, community-driven conference** for user experience professionals.

**Date:** June 9, 2018
**Venue:** Ijsfontein offices on Marineterrein, Amsterdam

---

## Format

BarCamp-style unconference with **28 sessions** across four time slots.

### Session Schedule

| Time | Parallel Sessions |
|------|------------------|
| 11:00 – 11:45 AM | 5 sessions |
| 12:00 – 12:45 PM | 6 sessions |
| 2:00 – 2:45 PM | 6 sessions |
| 3:00 – 3:45 PM | 6 sessions |

---

## Notable Session Topics

- Lovability — Design it, measure it
- Creating Magic: UX Design & Artificial Intelligence
- Blockchain Essentials
- Design Sprints and Customer Loyalty Canvas
- Cultural differences and design impact
- Empathy mapping and user research
