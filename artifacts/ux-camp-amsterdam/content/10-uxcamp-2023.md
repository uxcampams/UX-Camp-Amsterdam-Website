# UXcamp Amsterdam 2023

**Source URL:** https://uxcampams.com/uxcamp-ams-2023

---

## Overview

UXcamp Amsterdam is a **BarCamp around User Experience Design & Research** where the community shares knowledge and ideas.

> "There are no spectators, only contributors."

---

## Event Format

- One-day community-driven event
- Platform for sharing knowledge and stories about user experience
- All participants are contributors

---

## Session Topics

Sessions covered a wide range of UX topics including:
- Typography
- Continuous discovery
- Design leadership
- Systems perspective on design practice

Session materials and slides were shared with the community afterwards.
