## About the Event

UXcamp Amsterdam is an **open-for-all, participatory one-day BarCamp** where all content is generated and brought by its participants.

The organising team manages the **AmsterdamUX community**, which has:
- **5,700+ members**
- **190+ organised events**

The event encourages knowledge sharing in the UX field, welcoming designers, researchers, product managers, developers, and data scientists.
