# UXcamp Amsterdam 2024 — Post Event

**Source URL:** https://uxcampams.com/2024-post-event

---

## Overview

UXcamp Amsterdam 2024 was held on **June 29th** as a BarCamp format event for UX professionals, students, and enthusiasts from across Europe.

The organisers described it as **"truly special"** — a day of learning, sharing, and discussion.

---

## Key Details

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Date** | June 29, 2024 |
| **Venue** | University of Applied Sciences Amsterdam (Hogeschool van Amsterdam) |
| **Sessions** | 36 sessions |

---

## Session Topics Covered

- Content modelling
- Design systems
- Accessibility
- AI applications in UX
- Career development
- Automotive HMI design
- Impostor syndrome
- Design career progression
- Emerging technologies
